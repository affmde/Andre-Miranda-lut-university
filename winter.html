The cold season

The winter season is the coldest season of the year, which begins in the month of December and ends in the month of March. December and January are extreme winter months and are counted as the coldest months when temperatures in the northwestern regions of the country are around 10 to 15 ° C (mean 50 to 59 ° F), however, southeast.
